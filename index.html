Our Story

Who We Are

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO), with a single belief: that a compassionate society is built one act of kindness at a time.

Based in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, we are a Section 8 registered non-profit organisation licensed by the Central Government. Our work spans food distribution, education, women's empowerment, animal welfare, environmental conservation, and skill development across India.

We believe every underprivileged person deserves to grow with dignity and respect. Through collective action — volunteers, corporate partners, and individual supporters — we turn compassion into change.

What We Do

Our Ongoing Projects

Six flagship initiatives, each targeting a critical social need — working in unison to create lasting, holistic change.

🍲
Food & Clothing
Project Seva

Distributing nutritious food and warm clothing to underprivileged individuals and families. No one in our community should go to bed hungry or face the cold alone.

📚
Education
Project Bachpanshala

Bringing quality education to children who cannot afford it. We create safe, nurturing learning environments where every child can discover their potential.

🐾
Animal Welfare
Project Jeev

Rescuing, protecting, and feeding stray and injured animals. Compassion must extend beyond human borders — every living being deserves care.

🌸
Women Empowerment
Project Udaan

Equipping women with vocational skills and financial literacy to achieve economic independence, confidence, and leadership in their communities.

🌿
Environment
Project Prakriti

Planting trees, raising environmental awareness, and driving sustainability campaigns — protecting Mother Earth for generations to come.

🛠️
Skill Development
Project Vikas

Upskilling youth and young professionals through internship programmes and vocational training, enhancing employability across India. 30,000+ interns trained.

Dedicated Volunteers

🍚 Feeding Drives

Through Project Seva, our volunteers conduct regular food distribution drives ensuring that the hungry receive not just meals, but dignity and warmth alongside them.

📖 Educational Uplift

Bachpanshala has created safe learning spaces for underprivileged children, bridging the education gap and igniting hope for a brighter generation.

♀️ Women Rising

Project Udaan has helped women across multiple states build livelihoods of their own — from skill workshops to micro-enterprise support and financial empowerment.

🌳 Greening India

Project Prakriti volunteers have planted saplings across India, championing environmental sustainability and raising consciousness about our ecological responsibility.
